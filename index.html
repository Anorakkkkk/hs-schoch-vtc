About us

Built like a real VTC, not a generic template

HS Schoch Virtual is designed to feel like a proper trucking community: convoy-focused, driver-friendly and visually inspired by real road presence. The goal is to create a recognizable VTC with clean style and active participation.

Convoy Participation

Public convoys, community events and a structured presence on the road.

Professional Presentation

Clean branding, modern design and a VTC identity that stands out in TruckersMP.

Community Driven

Drivers, Discord activity and a relaxed but organized atmosphere.
