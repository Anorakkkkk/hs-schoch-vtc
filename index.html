About HS Schoch Virtual

HS Schoch Virtual is a modern ETS2 Virtual Trucking Company focused on professional convoy driving, community events and clean VTC presentation. Our goal is to create a friendly but organized environment where drivers can enjoy TruckersMP convoys and represent the VTC on the road.
